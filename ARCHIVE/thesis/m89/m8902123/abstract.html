以灰色理論為基礎之關聯式索引典應用於互動式查詢拓展

摘要

隨著全球資訊網上有利用性資訊越來越多，如何去有效的擷取資訊變的重要且合理的。查詢拓展是一種以建議詞補充原始查詢問句的程序可用來提昇查詢效率。假如查詢拓展的過程是採用與使用者互動的模式，則使用者與系統將會共同參與查詢拓展的工作，系統通常建議一些建議詞給使用者，而使用者從這些建議詞當中挑選一部分當作真正查詢拓展的拓展詞。一個設計良好的關聯式索引典不僅可以表達字詞間的語意關係更可以幫助搜尋引擎提昇檢索效能。而在過去的研究中，許多關聯式索引典被以對稱性來表達，這也造成許多不合理的結果。 因此，在我們的研究中，我們提出一種與使用者互動的查詢模式，目的是提供使用者一個較容易的方法去獲得他們所要的資訊。而利用我們所提出的一種新的灰色關聯係數計算的演算法所產生的不具對稱性的關聯式索引典也被應用在查詢拓展的程序中。並且，這個新的灰關聯係數計算的演算法改良了傳統灰色關聯係數計算中無法處理稀疏矩陣的問題而且具有降低雜訊詞的能力。我們成功的應用此種能處理稀疏矩陣的灰色關聯係數演算法來建立一個不具對稱性的關聯式索引典用於互動式的查詢拓展。 在我們的實驗中顯示，以關聯式索引典輔助查詢拓展的查詢模式能夠些微的提昇檢索效益，我們也發現許多影響關聯式索引典品質的因素，如斷詞的精確度與資料及的完整性等，因此有關關聯式索引典建構與查詢問句拓展研究的未來發展，扔然有許多問題尚需解決。

Association Thesaurus Based on Grey Theory for Interactive Query Expansion

Abstract

With the increasing availability of information on the WWW (World Wide Web), it becomes more important and feasible to retrieve information efficiently. Query expansion is the process of supplementing an original query with additional terms in order to refine a search and increase retrieval effectiveness. If the query expansion is interactive, then the user and the system work together to expand the query. A well-designed thesaurus not only can represent the semantic knowledge among terms, but also can help to improve the retrieval performance in search engine. In previous researches, many thesauruses are represented as symmetric type, and this lead to some unreasonable results. Therefore, in this study, we propose an interactive searching scheme that aims to provide users an easy way to retrieve their desired information. A non-symmetric association thesaurus, which is generated by Sparse Grey Relational Analysis (SGRA) algorithm we proposed, is involved in query expansion process. Also, the SGRA algorithm improves the problem that traditional grey relational analysis cannot deal with sparse matrix, and to reduce the noise term. This is, sparse grey relation analysis is successfully applied to construct a non-symmetric association thesaurus for interactive query expansion. Experiment show that the searching scheme of expanding query by association thesaurus has improved retrieval performance slightly. We also find that the correctness of word segmentation and the completeness of dataset have a great impact on the quality of association thesaurus. Therefore, for the further study of association thesaurus construction and query expansion, there are still many problems to be solved.
